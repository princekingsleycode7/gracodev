About the Programme

Built for Abia.
Built for You.

Grassroots Community Development Initiative (GRACODEV), in partnership with Prevailing Computers Limited, is facilitating free digital certificates in Graphics Design, Web Development, and Cybersecurity to all Nigerians in Abia State.

We believe that access to digital skills should not be a privilege. This programme is designed to empower individuals who hope to foster change in their trajectory for financial freedom and enhanced productivity across diverse fields.

Frequently Asked Questions

Got Questions?
We've Got Answers.

Is the training really 100% free?
▼
Yes — completely free. No registration fee, no tuition, no hidden charges. The programme is fully funded by GRACODEV in partnership with Prevailing Computers Limited as a grassroots community development initiative for Abia State.
Who is eligible to apply?
▼
Any Nigerian currently residing in Abia State is eligible, regardless of age, educational background, or employment status. We welcome students, graduates, working professionals, and those currently seeking employment.
Will I receive a certificate at the end?
▼
Yes! Upon successful completion of your chosen course, you will be awarded an official digital skills certificate that you can use in your portfolio, for job applications, or to attract freelance clients.
Can I apply for more than one course?
▼
Yes, you may indicate your preferred course(s) during registration. Course availability and scheduling may affect whether you can undertake multiple courses simultaneously. Our team will guide you during the confirmation stage.
Where will the training take place?
▼
Training venue details and schedules will be communicated to successful applicants via SMS and WhatsApp after your application is reviewed. Please ensure your contact details are correct during registration.
Do I need prior ICT experience?
▼
Not at all! Our programmes are designed to welcome complete beginners and those with existing knowledge alike. Training starts from the foundational level and builds progressively to give you confidence and real-world skills.
